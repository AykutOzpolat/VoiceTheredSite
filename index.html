Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

What we collect

When you create an account, we store your display name, email address, and a securely hashed password. We do not collect or store any other personal information.

How we use your email

Your email is used exclusively for:

  • Account verification upon registration
  • Password reset requests

We do not send marketing emails, newsletters, or promotional content. All emails are triggered by your own actions.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely on AWS infrastructure (DynamoDB, S3) in the EU (Stockholm). Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text.

Third-party sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. We use Amazon SES solely for delivering transactional emails.

Data deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at support@voicethered.com.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach us at support@voicethered.com.
